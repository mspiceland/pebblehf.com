Pebble HF

An ultra-affordable HF radio for anyone.

Why?

Amateur radio is a fantastic hobby for learning, connection, and adventure. Many people get into amateur radio with inexpensive voice radios and a tech license. Now anyone looking to explore the wonderful world of HF radio will now have an ultra-affordable option. Now upgrade to your general license and start having fun!

Small and portable radios are perfect for learning HF radio, connecting with people, and outdoor radio adventures. They allow you to have fun with the expanding world of Parks on the Air and Summits on the Air. Pebble HF is an ultra-affordable, ultra-portable HF radio kit for anyone to get into HF amateur radio, to open up those possibilities.

Pebble HF is an ultra affordable kit that is easy to build, to get more people into the hobby and having fun.

Check out the backstory

What makes Pebble HF different?

  • Ultra-affordable under $50 and easy to build
  • ~ 5W output with 12V input
  • ~ 1W output powered by USB-C 5V
  • Built in CW key and microphone

From the begining, every decision was made to keep the cost low and easy to build. Pebble HF was born from the idea of how to make a capable QRP radio ultra-affordable and easy to build. For that reason, it is a single band, minimal parts, uses as many surface mount parts fully populated, and is kit-friendly. It uses an existing and well proven design, the uSDX, which suites these goals.

Pebble HF is a QRP all-mode capable radio for a single band. Pebble HF is a low power QRP radio that can output ~5W on battery power but can also output ~1W powered by USB, so that you can get started even without investing a special battery yet. Weighing less than 0.5 lbs (8oz), it is a great option for trying out portable radio.

Pebble HF is an open source design based on the classic uSDX. Hardware design by Barb WB2CBA and firmware by Guido PE1NNZ and modified by Mike N4FFF.

* digital modes requires an external adapter.

Features

  • Ultra-affordable, single band, all mode capable radio kit for HF ham radio
  • CW, SSB (USB/LSB), AM, and digital mode capable*
    (* an external usb sound card is required for digital modes)
  • Band: 20 meters (40 meter version may be available in the future)
  • Up to 5W output (7-14V input) or 1W on USB-C (5V)
  • DSP filters: full open, 2500, 1700, 500, 200, 100, and 50 Hz
  • AGC and noise reduction
  • VOX (auto transmit for digital modes)
  • Open hardware + firmware
  • Kit-friendly build (most parts pre-soldered)
  • Weighs less than 0.5 lbs (8oz)
  • Power consumption: ~80mA RX, 500-600mA TX @ 12V
  • Class E design

* digital modes requires an external adapter.

Build

Pebble HF comes as a kit that is uniquely easy to build.

With almost all parts pre-soldered and ready to go, there are only 10 easy through-hole parts to install. Most people are able to completely build the radio in less than an hour.

Backstory

Pebble HF was born out of passion from HamRadioDuo, in collaboration with Barb wb2cba, as a way to give back. Mike, N4FFF, may not have gotten into the hobby without originaly thinking that a tiny $10 kit was all he needed. It turns out, he was never able to make a contact on it, but he was hooked. Now we want to make sure that a great option exists for anyone wanting to get started. Mike N4FFF and Becky N4BKY have now met many people who want to get started, but are not ready to jump into the deep end. They are not yet sure how much money or time they want to commit to try morse code or outdoor radio or HF radio in general. After you learn and realize it is for you, your budget may increase. There are many fantastic QPR radios already (QMX and CFT1 are great examples). If you are already interested in one of those, please get one! Pebble HF was created for all of those people who need an ultra low cost option with minimal effort to build as a kit.
